Q&A

What is Rhythm Deck?

An AI-powered platform for music creators to generate lyrics, videos, and engage fans.

Is it really free?

Yes! The Free Plan includes unlimited creative tools and public uploads.

What do I get with Pro?

Pro unlocks sharing tools, fan polls, live streaming, and eligibility for Main Stage & Featured Artist slots.

How do I upgrade?

You can upgrade anytime from your profile admin page or during signup.

How do I get on the Main Stage or Featured artist?

Artists can apply or be nominated. Criteria: originality, quality, engagement, and visuals. Final approval by staff. Updated monthly.

Can I collaborate with other artists?

Yes! Connect with others in the Rhythm Deck community to collaborate on music and visuals.

Can fans interact with my music?

Pro users can host polls, Q&A, and fan shoutouts directly from their artist page.

Are there limits to uploads?

Free users have limited file sizes, while Pro users get higher storage and priority uploads.

When are new features added?

We release new AI and community features monthly based on user feedback.

Can I monetize my content?

Pro users will soon be able to earn from fan tips and sponsorship integrations.
