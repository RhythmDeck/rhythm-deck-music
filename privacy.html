Privacy Policy

At Rhythm Deck Music, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email, and password when you sign up.
  • User Content: Music, videos, images, and other materials you upload.
  • Analytics Data: Basic usage data to help us improve our services (e.g., page views, session duration).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage and personalize your account experience.
  • To display and promote your uploaded content within Rhythm Deck.
  • To communicate updates, new features, and community news (if you opt in).
  • To ensure platform security and prevent abuse.

3. Data Protection

We use industry-standard encryption and secure hosting services to protect your data. Your password is encrypted, and we never sell or share personal data with third parties for profit.

4. Cookies

Rhythm Deck uses cookies to remember your login session and improve performance. You can manage cookie settings through your browser preferences.

5. Third-Party Services

Some analytics or media storage tools may use third-party integrations. These services follow their own privacy policies and are selected carefully to ensure compliance with global privacy standards.

6. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of promotional communications at any time.
  • Request account deletion via our contact form.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The “Last Updated” date below will always reflect the most recent version.
